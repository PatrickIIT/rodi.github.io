05 — Business Model

Free for citizens. Paid by institutions.

RODI is always free for every Tanzanian. The revenue comes from the banks, employers, and fintechs who benefit most from a financially legible youth population.

The Core Logic

Tanzania has 17M+ youth with no unified tax engagement platform. Every bank, employer, fintech, and telco wants verified access to this audience. TRA has the mandate. RODI is the bridge — and the bridge charges a toll to the institutions, not the people.

This mirrors the credit bureau model — but built on tax compliance (a positive signal) rather than debt history. In a cash economy, this is far more powerful and inclusive.

09 — Risk & Mitigation

Know the risks. Own the answers.

Every strong idea has risks. These are the most significant — and exactly how RODI handles them.

High — Adoption
Youth don't register or don't stay active after the first month
Mitigation: Lead with burudani, not civic messaging. First RODI benefit must be felt within 2 weeks of joining. 1,000 campus and mtaa ambassador programme activates peer pressure. Streak mechanics punish inactivity with visible score decay.
High — Institutional
Banks and fintechs don't integrate the RODI Score API
Mitigation: Phase 1 builds the Score in isolation — no partner dependency. Phase 2 launches with 2–3 anchor partners (CRDB, Nala) who co-design the product. Pilot data showing compliance-to-creditworthiness correlation is the sales tool.
Medium — Technology
USSD / mobile gaps in rural Tanzania limit reach
Mitigation: Phase 1 is urban only (Dar, Dodoma, Mwanza). USSD ensures no smartphone dependency for core compliance. Offline point-earning at physical TRA offices and partner locations. Rural rollout begins Phase 3 with improved infrastructure.
Medium — Commercial
Partner revenue doesn't materialise at projected scale
Mitigation: Government SaaS revenue (TRA licence fee) covers core operating costs regardless of partner uptake. 18-month minimum contracts at Dhahabu tier. Monthly ROI reports proving acquisition cost vs. market rate provide retention argument.
Low — Fraud
Youth game the system to earn XP without genuine compliance
Mitigation: NIDA-verified identity prevents duplicate accounts. All compliance actions are cross-verified with TRA's IDRAS in real time — you can't fake a filed return. High-value Score milestones require 3+ months of verified history.
Low — Privacy
Youth data misuse, breach, or misappropriation
Mitigation: All data is sovereign — stored in Tanzania, custodied by TRA. Partners receive Score queries only, never raw compliance data. Consent is explicit, granular, and revocable at any time. No data is sold; partners pay for API calls, not profiles.
